About Us

We Are Local

Longfellow☉Life is a private not-for-profit service in the Longfellow neighborhood of Minneapolis, Minnesota. All information provided on this website is provided for your benefit without warranty, as is. News & Information external resources focus on local community culture and small business.

Free Books

With Little Free Library we offer free books from our private collection, neighborhood donors, and other charitable sources. We regularly freshen and rotate selections spanning fiction, nonfiction, reference, young adult and children's hard and soft cover books. Books in the Little Free Library section are indexed using Library Thing.
